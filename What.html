Our Work

We work with the RUET donor database to track and calculate the last donation date for each donor. This ensures that no one donates blood before 90 days have passed, maintaining the safety and health of our donors and recipients.

Our system carefully verifies donation intervals, manages donor records, and provides timely notifications for safe blood donation. By maintaining accurate records and enforcing donation guidelines, we help keep the blood donation process safe, efficient, and reliable for everyone involved.
